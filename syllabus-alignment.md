## Topic Selection Guidelines

Students must select research topics that:

1. **Align with Official Curriculum**: Topics must correspond to at least one unit in the syllabus alignment matrix above.

2. **Demonstrate Appropriate Depth**: Research must go beyond superficial treatment and engage with theoretical foundations, practical applications, and current research.

3. **Show Academic Rigor**: Topics must be supported by peer-reviewed academic sources, technical standards, or authoritative industry publications.

4. **Address Current Relevance**: Topics should reflect contemporary issues, emerging technologies, or evolving best practices in computer engineering.

## Consultation Process

Students uncertain about topic selection or syllabus alignment should:

1. Review this alignment document thoroughly
2. Identify the most relevant unit and learning outcomes
3. Prepare a brief topic proposal (200-300 words)
4. Schedule consultation with course instructor or teaching assistant
5. Obtain approval before beginning detailed research
